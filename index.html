About Us

Food Security. We provide food 365 days Our purpose is to provide access to low cost high-quality food. The farm365 story starts inside a farm we visited at a vegetable cluster in a rural community where we saw a landfilled of wasted vegetable. It got our founders Abdulaziz and Bello, thinking about why such huge waste, why was there no solution, what was cause of the waste. They start a journey to find out why the waste become a re-occurring situation annually. In fact a shocking 40% of produce is wasted before it even leaves the farms, these means a lot of unnecessary waste for the planet. Growers suffers from a huge loss and the society couldn’t feed itself Visited farmers in rural communities to battle food waste and make food available and affordable. Talked to growers and come up with the idea for FARM365
Farm365 vegetable produce delivered straight to your door.
